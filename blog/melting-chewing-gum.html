Melting a chewing gum

On a fine weekday morning, I decided to chew gum while being bored in an organic chemistry class. It is perfectly alright to do so, according to the moral police. Chewing gum is a promised way to keep you awake and alert(maybe). But the interesting class had to end sometime. One of my friends pulled out a chocolate on the way out and offered me a share(generosity). I took it in without throwing out the chewing gum. I wanted to continue chewing the gum.

Lo and behold! The chewing gum melts and goes in with the chocolate. That was a moment full of horror. The possibility of molten gum hardening along my oesophagus scared the hell out of me. What's more? My friends didn't believe me. They assumed I swallowed it and am under the illusion that it melted. I needed help to prove my point.

Googling revealed that cocoa butter is an emulsifier for gum base. This trick may not work on all gums, as bases are different, but perfectly tested and discovered for Happydent brand gums.
