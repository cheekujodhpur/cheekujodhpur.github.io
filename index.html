Magic is a myth, magicians not.

My magical staff is called an undergraduate degree in Physics. It is embedded with various power gems; I am a part of the National Astronomy Olympiad programme, and have worked on a few astronomy projects. I have done freelance web development and undergraduate research in computer graphics. Additionally, I managed the Web and Coding Club at IIT Bombay. These days I am an everyday data scientist.
